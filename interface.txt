the number in the second row define the interface model as follow


1:#linear
c=sin(q*3^(0.5))/(q*3^(0.5))

2:#step
c=cos(q)

3:#exponential
c=1/(1+q^2/2)

4:#error function
c=exp(-q^2/2)

see D.G.Stearns et al, "Nonspecular x-ray scattering in a multilayer-coated imaging system", Journal of Applied Physics, vol 84, N.2, 15 July 1998.
